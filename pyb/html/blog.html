December 9, 2016 - Mount Pleasant, MI


Pack Your Back Delivers Holiday Surprises




Michigan teachers received an early holiday gift this December thanks to the Pack Your Back organization. The nonprofit, dedicated to “supplying success,” teamed up with a student group, known as Advocates for Justice in Education, at Central Michigan University in order to put together a holiday-themed charity event: “Stuff Your Stocking.”


The cause was simple: to ensure that teachers in Michigan would have the proper resources in order to succeed in the classroom for the second semester of school. To accomplish this, “Stuff Your Stocking” distributed decorated holiday stockings filled with new school supplies to both the Huron Learning Center in Bad Axe, and five public elementary schools in Mount Pleasant.







This is the second major event Pack Your Back has put on, the first being a summer back-to-school school supply distribution in Flint, Michigan.

According to Pack Your Back founder, Galen Miller, the organization was looking to expand their efforts beyond the start of the school year. “We don’t want our distributions to only occur during the summer right before school,” explained Miller. “We want to help students year round in getting the resources they need.”

The event was much appreciated by the recipients. "Thank you so much for the stockings full of school supplies. The pens, pencils, tape, markers, crayons, rulers, and the rest will all be put to good use with our students. Thank you for your kindness and generosity. You have made a difference in our students' lives." - Huron Learning Center. Due to the program’s success, Pack Your Back looks forward to implementing it in the future.



Pack Your Back at CMU



Pack Your Back is a nonprofit organization that was started in a dorm room in Calkins Hall. It’s a few students at CMU who decided that they wanted to be hands on, they wanted to be proactive in helping the younger generation succeed. With that, Pack Your Back was born.


In January 2016, Galen Miller was volunteering in Flint with his fraternity. As they continued to deliver water and other supplies to the residents suffering from the water crisis, Miller noticed that as the major media outlets began to leave, so did the help.


That night after the event in Flint, Miller got together a group of friends in his dorm room, and they discussed how they could continue to give back to the community in Flint. They settled on collecting and donating school supplies, as they realized that the water crisis had a massive impact on the development of young school-aged children.

A group of CMU students and alumni started Pack Your Back in March 2016 and were officially approved as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in May. They set to work collecting funds and school supplies to donate to the children of Flint and beyond.

The organization plans to extend to the rest of the state and even the country in the following months and years and already has an expansion plan in place.









23% of Michigan fall below the poverty line. There is a constant need for education supplies to help children succeed.





Why Donate?



Pack Your Back relies on the kind donations from the community and various businesses in order to provide resources for children to succeed in the classroom. Since there are always more children in need, Pack Your Back is always in need of donations to continue providing students with resources.
