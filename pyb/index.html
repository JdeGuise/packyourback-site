Who We Are

Pack Your Back is a nonprofit organization that was established in 2016 after organizers noticed the financial burden families have been facing after the collapse of the economy and the recent decline of the United States school system.

More recently, the Flint Water Crisis has created health concerns for children in Flint, Michigan. Pack Your Back is currently working to supply the children of Flint, Michigan with the supplies needed to succeed in the classroom.

What We Do

Pack Your Back raises charitable donations - school supplies such as backpacks, pencils, funds, etc. - in order to distribute them to impoverished communities in need across greater Michigan.

"This is an inspiring tagline, that concisely shows the impact that we're making" - John deGuise

Why Donate?

Pack Your Back relies on the kind donations from the community and various stores in order to provide the school supplies to the children. Since there are always more children in need, our goal this year is to donate over 500 backpacks filled with school supplies.

In addition to donations of backpacks and other school supplies, the Pack Your Back movement is relying on sponsorships from local businesses and organizations to fund the cause. Click the link below for more information.
